# react-mvvm-component

Build React apps faster with "modern MVVM" architecture.

Fast and lightweight(6KB), **supports React 17~19**, works with existing components, elegant.

## Release Notice

Due to previous instances in the Taiwanese open-source community where proper attribution to original authors was not always observed, a partially open-source approach has been adopted after careful consideration of various factors. The project remains freely available, with the aim of preserving the spirit of original authorship while upholding the principles of free software.

Whether the full source code will be made publicly available in the future remains undecided, and this direction will continue to be evaluated based on a variety of factors. However, community feedback and support are highly valued. If the **project reaches 10K stars, a commitment will be made to introduce more practical features and to continue releasing future versions under a free license as a way to give back to the community.**

Until then, maintenance will be focused on addressing critical bugs and major security issues to ensure a stable and secure user experience.

Thank you for your understanding and support. Ongoing interest and participation from the community remain the key driving forces behind the continued development of this project.
